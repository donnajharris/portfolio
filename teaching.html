Teaching

I have worked professionally as a college instructor (computer science), but I have been teaching all my life. It is part of my nature is to help others and to share information. Even as a child I was helping and teaching peers and adults alike.

I started teaching music as a young teen, both privately and through church programs. Beginning in my twenties, I started teaching through Salvation Army music camps and also took on educational positions during my undergraduate studies at the University of Guelph.

I bring a teacher's heart to every role and many skills which been grown and expanded simply for the joy of sharing with others.

Video: Why Does My Text Keep Changing?!

Instructional Video.

Why Does My Text Keep Changing?! How to Make Autocorrect Work for You!

In this scenario-based how-to video, I walk through the ins and outs of why some applications change text on us when we type. It demonstrates:

  • various ways that two popular word processors (Microsoft Word and Google Docs) alter text while we work
  • where to adjust settings for these alterations
  • how to work around text replacements we don't want
  • how to create our own text substitutions

I used Camtasia 2019 and REAPER for video and audio editing. The thumbnail and end cards were created using Adobe Express.
